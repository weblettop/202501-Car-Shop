BMW

The history of BMW began in 1916 with the founding of Rapp Motorenwerke, a company that manufactured aircraft engines. In 1917, it was renamed Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW), which translates as "Bavarian Motor Works". Initially, the company manufactured aircraft engines, then switched to producing motorcycles and cars.
